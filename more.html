If you still do not see a list of contacts in the Address Book, continue to the following step-by-step guide:

Step: 1

On Outlook’s bottom left, click on Contacts.

Step: 2

On your top left, look for “Contacts – YOUR EMAIL.”


Step: 3

Step 3: Right click it and select Properties.

Step: 4

Click on Outlook Address Book tab and tick on “Show this folder as an e-mail Address Book”.


Step: 5

Make sure to select the right Contact List in your Address Book.
